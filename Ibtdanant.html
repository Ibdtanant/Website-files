    Why US?

    Questions handpicked by Toppers

    There’s a barrage of questions in the market and that
    confuses an aspirant leading to wastage of time. Ibtdanant
    offers a library of questions that are proven to increase
    the problem-solving ability of the student.

    Concept clearing solutions

    Solutions provided by Ibtdanant focuses on answering the What, Why, and How of the questions instead of just giving the answers.

    AI/ML Integrated test series

    Considering the PSA of every individual, we provide a machine learning based test series that adapts its difficulty level according to the foregoing question the student has solved

    Performance analysis report

    Ibtdanant provides a descriptive analysis of every member to monitor his/her learning growth, making every individual aware of his/her strong and weak concepts

    Student standing Index

    According to the user data, a ranking and reward system is created to imbibe a healthy competitive spirit among the test-takers, helping them enhance their performance

    Student adaptive community

    Ibtdanant provides the best concept-building questions and solutions. In addition to that, there’s a student community to update the database according to the needs of the students.
